Lakeview Garden Chinese Restaurant
The best Chinese Food in Conejo Valley for over 20 years

We are a family run business serving Conejo Valley with delicious take-out, delivery, and dine-in accompanied with the best customer service. Lakeview Garden wants to thank every single customer that has supported us throughout the years, especially during COVID-19
